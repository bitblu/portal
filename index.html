Why Bitblu?

Crypto-currency investment is hard...

Our mission is to make your digital investment experience easy and secure.

To invest smartly, you should know the meaning of terms like Litecoin, PPCoin, Ripple and ASICMiner. Bitblu builds simple indexes and investment tools that turns this complex world into something as easy to understand as the S&P 500 or Google Finance.

Transparency means everything to us

We will set the bar for transparency in the Bitcoin world.

It is important to us that you understand each component of your investment portfolio, and will be able to rest assured knowing how your funds are doing. We promise to never play dirty, and be completely honest and open about what do and how we do it.
